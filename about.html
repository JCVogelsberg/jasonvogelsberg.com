About

I'm J. Vogelsberg, an aspiring web developer living in Portland, Oregon. Originally from Louisville, Kentucky, I have spent the last decade living in Florida. Enjoying a wide range of interests, I started out as a journalism major, then switched to an Aviation Maintenance degree, graduating from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University in 2009. I worked in Light Sport aviation, helicopters, and Part-161 flight training operations before deciding I wanted a new technical challenge. This brought me first to web development, then to Oregon.

When I'm not messing around in front of my MacBook, I enjoy buiding and rallying Hondas and Subarus, reading, drinking ales, drinking tea, writing, studying history, and sailing.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @J_Vogelsberg.
