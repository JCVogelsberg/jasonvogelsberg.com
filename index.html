A Little About Me...

I am a front end web developer living in Portland, Oregon. In 2014, I graduated from Epicodus, an intensive, four-month web development bootcamp, where I studied HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript/jQuery, Ruby/Rails, PostgreSQL, and Ember.js. Since graduating, I have studied Photoshop, Wordpress (specializing in the Genesis framework), and the LAMP stack.

When I'm not messing around in front of my MacBook, I enjoy classic Japanese trucks, reading, archery, ham radio, drinking ales, drinking tea, artisanal ballistics, writing, salmon and trout fishing, studying history, and sailing.
